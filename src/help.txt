Welcome to the Mindset Application!

This application is designed to help you boost your mood, motivation, and overall mindset by providing you with inspiring and motivational quotes. Here's a quick overview of what you can do:

1. Surprise me with a random quote: Get a random quote to uplift your spirits.

2. Boost your mood: Get a quote based on your specific need or category preference.

3. Quote Options: Create your own quotes, remove existing ones, or edit quotes.

4. Search Quotes: Find quotes containing specific keywords or phrases.

5. Category Options: View all existing categories, create new ones, or remove existing ones.

6. Help: View this help message for a quick guide on how to use the application.

7. Exit: Close the Mindset Application.

Feel free to explore the various features of the application and let it inspire you on your journey towards a positive mindset!

If you have any questions or encounter any issues, please don't hesitate to reach out for assistance.

Enjoy the Mindset Application!
